# Hippie Films — UI Reference

Updated: 2026-05-25 Asia/Jerusalem

## Visual Source

Owner supplied existing business card images:

- Front: black star field, gold handwritten "Hippie films" logo.
- Back/contact side: Hebrew service line, phone, email, Instagram/Facebook handle, gold text on black star field.

This card is the visual source of truth for the landing page.

## Brand Tokens

| Token | Value |
|---|---|
| Background | `#030302`, `#080704`, star-field black |
| Gold primary | `#f0c66a` |
| Warm gold glow | `#d69b3d` |
| Cream text | `#fff4d2` |
| Muted text | `#d6c49a` |
| Card glass | `rgba(10, 8, 5, 0.72)` |
| Border | `rgba(240, 198, 106, 0.28)` |
| Focus ring | `#f6d98a` |

## Typography Direction

- Main logo feeling: handwritten/script gold signature.
- Body and buttons: clean sans-serif for readability.
- Hebrew service line: elegant, spaced, not too heavy.
- Avoid huge corporate headings that fight the business-card feel.

## Layout Direction

| Tier | Standard |
|---|---|
| Hero | Full-screen star field, centered/low logo, gold glow, immediate actions |
| Primary action | WhatsApp and email as large gold/black touch cards |
| Social action | Instagram/Facebook prominent, TikTok/Sanpaku/YouTube/Vimeo secondary |
| Service cards | Small, elegant, 2-column desktop / stacked mobile |
| QR notes | Mostly docs/README, not dominant for visitors |

## CTA Hierarchy

1. Primary: WhatsApp / Send Email.
2. Secondary: Instagram / Facebook / Watch work.
3. Tertiary: YouTube / Vimeo archive / Sanpaku / TikTok.

## Mobile Rules

- Design first for iPhone width.
- Touch targets at least 44px.
- Do not crowd the first screen.
- Keep important copy readable over the star background.
- Avoid text over bright star clusters.
- Keep QR black on white with clear quiet zone when generated.

## What Not To Do

- Do not make it look like generic Linktree.
- Do not use unrelated HippieFilms.in or Indian contact details.
- Do not place final QR on a busy background.
- Do not invent services, awards, clients, or portfolio claims.
- Do not overload the page with long explanations.

## Design Definition Of Done

- The page feels like the existing card became interactive.
- The first viewport shows brand identity and clear contact actions.
- All links are editable from one data file.
- The page works cleanly on mobile.
- QR print guidance remains available but does not dominate the visitor experience.
