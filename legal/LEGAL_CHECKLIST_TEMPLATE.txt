Legal / Privacy Checklist

Use when a task touches:
- personal data
- health data
- location
- payments
- user-generated content
- children/minors
- suppliers/contracts
- public sharing
- deletion/export

Questions:
1. What data is collected?
2. Why is it needed?
3. Where is it stored?
4. Who can access it?
5. Is consent needed?
6. Does copy/UI make a legal claim?
7. Are Terms/Privacy/Support docs affected?
8. Is owner approval required?
